About me

I'm a postdoctoral researcher in mathematics working at IHES with Fanny Kassel starting in September 2025. Before that, since October 2024, I have been postdoc at LMJL in Nantes, France, within the ANR project ADYCT. I completed my PhD in the Spectral Analysis group in Paderborn, Germany, under supervision of Tobias Weich.

Research

My main research interest lies in
  • Spectral theory, in particular of multiple commuting operators
  • Discrete subgroups of Lie groups, in particular in higher rank
  • Harmonic analysis on (locally) symmetric spaces
  • Quantum ergodicity
