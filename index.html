About me

I'm a postdoctoral researcher in mathematics working at IHES with Fanny Kassel starting in September 2025. Before that, since October 2024, I was postdoc in Nantes, France, at LMJL in the ANR project ADYCT. I did my PhD in the Spectral Analysis group in Paderborn, Germany, under supervision of Tobias Weich.

Research

My main research lies in spectral analysis, in particular spectral geometry. I'm specifically interested in symmetric settings such as (locally) homogeneous or symmetric spaces, where I study situations with multiple commuting operators.
